THE FESTIVAL

THE COMEBACK EXPERIENCE

JUST A FEW MINUTES FROM THE CENTER OF UTRECHT, THE COMEBACK FESTIVAL BRINGS TOGETHER ROCK LEGENDS AND FANS FROM ALL OVER THE WORLD FOR AN UNFORGETTABLE WEEKEND. THE FESTIVAL TAKES PLACE IN A WIDE OPEN SPACE SURROUNDED BY NATURE, DESIGNED TO RELIVE THE GREATEST MOMENTS IN ROCK HISTORY WHILE CREATING NEW MEMORIES FILLED WITH ENERGY AND EMOTION.

MAIN STAGE - LEGENDS

THE HEART OF THE COMEBACK BEATS ON THE MAIN STAGE, AN IMPRESSIVE OPEN-AIR SPACE READY TO HOST THE 6 LEGENDARY PERFORMANCES. THESE ARTISTS WILL BE SPREAD ACROSS THE 3 DAYS OF THE FESTIVAL AND WILL ALWAYS PLAY IN THE MORNING. (THE EXACT SCHEDULE IS NOT YET AVAILABLE).

THE CHILL ZONE

THIS IS WHERE FANS CAN TAKE A BREAK, GRAB MERCHANDISE, VINYL SHOPS AND CHILL AREAS INSPIRED BY CLASSIC ROCK AESTHETICS. IT'S THE PERFECT PLACE TO RELAX, MEET PEOPLE AND RECHARGE BEFORE THE NEXT CONCERT.

SILVER ZONE - VIP

IF YOU BOOKED THE SILVER TICKET, YOU HAVE A PRIVILEGED ACCESS TO THE CLOSEST VIEWING ZONES, PREMIUM DRINKS AND A GOURMET FOOD SELECTION. THIS PASS ALSO INCLUDES FAST ENTRY TO THE FESTIVAL AND ACCESS TO AN EXCLUSIVE TERRACE OVERLOOKING THE MAIN STAGE.

NIGHT SESSIONS

WHEN THE NIGHT FALLS, THE ATMOSPHERE TRANSFORMS. LIGHT SHOWS, TRIBUTE PERFORMANCES AND DJ SETS TAKE OVER THE STAGE, BLENDING TIMELESS ROCK ANTHEMS WITH MODERN SOUNDS. THE MUSIC NEVER STOPS, AND THE ENERGY ONLY GROWS STRONGER.
