The Comeback is not just another music festival, it’s a celebration of rock history. For one unforgettable weekend, legends like Dire Straits, Scorpions or Guns N’ Roses will reunite on stage to bring back the energy, passion and sound that defined generations.

Set in the open fields of Utrecht, this once-in-a-lifetime event promises powerful performances, emotional reunions and unexpected surprises. For some artists, it’s a long-awaited return; for others, perhaps a final goodbye. But for everyone who attends, it will be a memory that lasts forever.

The Comeback blends nostalgia with a modern experience, spectacular lights, cutting-edge staging and an atmosphere where rock’s golden era meets today’s spirit.

Join thousands of fans from around the world for a weekend of guitars, fireworks and timeless songs. Because legends never truly fade, they just wait for their comeback.
