HOW TO GET HERE

UTRECHT IS AN ACCESSIBLE DESTINATION PERFECTLY CONNECTED TO THE REST OF EUROPE. WHETHER YOU'RE ARRIVING BY PLANE, TRAIN, OR CAR, GETTING TO THE HOME OF THE COMEBACK FESTIVAL IS QUICK AND EASY. HERE'S YOUR ULTIMATE GUIDE TO REACH THE CENTER OF THE MUSIC AND LIVE AN UNFORGETTABLE WEEKEND SURROUNDED BY LEGENDS AND PURE ROCK ENERGY.

BY CAR / BUS

DRIVE TO EUROPALAAN 55, 3526 KP UTRECHT. ON-SITE PARKING WILL BE AVAILABLE AND CLEARLY SIGNPOSTED. PUBLIC BUSES (QBUZZ / U-OV) RUN FROM UTRECHT CENTRAAL TO NEARBY STOPS, WITH FESTIVAL SHUTTLES ON EVENT DAYS.

BY TRAIN

TRAINS CONNECT UTRECHT WITH ALL MAJOR DUTCH AND EUROPEAN CITIES. FROM UTRECHT CENTRAAL, REACH THE VENUE EASILY BY TAXI OR PUBLIC BUS. DIRECT TRAINS FROM SCHIPHOL RUN FREQUENTLY.

BY PLANE

FLY TO AMSTERDAM SCHIPHOL AIRPORT (AMS). TAKE A DIRECT TRAIN TO UTRECHT CENTRAAL (30–35 MIN). FROM THERE, GRAB A TAXI OR LOCAL BUS TO EUROPALAAN 55.
